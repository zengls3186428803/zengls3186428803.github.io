$R$是一个有序环，则可以在$R$上定义绝对值

$|x|=\begin{cases}x, & x \geq 0 \\ -x, & x < 0\end{cases}$

定义距离$d(x,y)=|x-y|$

#### 性质

1. $|x| \geq 0$
2. $|-x|=|x|$
3. $|x|=0 \Leftrightarrow x=0$
4. $x<|x|$
5. $|x+y| \leq |x|+|y|$
6. $|xy|=|x||y|$
7. $d(x, y) \geq 0$. Also, $d(x, y) = 0$ if and only if $x = y.$
8. $d(x, y) = d(y, x)$.
9. $d(x, z) ≤ d(x, y) + d(y, z)$.
